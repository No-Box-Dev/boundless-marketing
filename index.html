The NoBox story

From a family's need to a platform that grows with every child.

Chapter One

The walls around our plans

We wanted to live in different parts of the world — but education became the invisible fence around our lives. Our intention was not to exclude schools, but to ensure a strong and consistent educational foundation so that the quality of education our children receive is not dependent on a single institution.

Chapter Two

We searched

We searched across many online platforms, hoping to find one suitable solution or a combination of platforms that could offer the structure and continuity we needed. Unfortunately, we couldn't find it. Did that mean we had to give up on our dream?..or...

Chapter Three

If you cannot find it, build it

So we began researching different educational models and analyzing thousands of exercises corresponding with IGCSE, IB, and a range of learning methodologies. We worked intensively to translate those insights into a practical solution. NoBox Education is the result.

How to get started

Three steps to a personalised learning journey designed around your child.

1

Tell us about your family

Complete a short intake about your child — their age, interests, and what matters to you. No commitments, just context.

2

Speak with an education coach

A real conversation — not a chatbot. We discuss your child's learning style, personality, subjects, and whether you'd like a matched tutor, your own, or to guide it yourself.

3

Start learning — anywhere

Your child receives a personalised, science-based program aligned with international curricula. With a tutor, learning is guided together. Without one, the platform adapts and leads.

See it in action

Child dashboard

A safe, distraction-free learning space your child can navigate independently — no ads, no random links, no unnecessary clutter.

  • Visually engaging without distracting games or animations
  • Children work independently without constant help
  • Subjects with real-time progress tracking
  • Daily streaks and rewards that motivate without distracting
  • Personal avatar and customisable themes

Adaptive exercises

Real pedagogy, not random worksheets. Exercises adjust to your child's level so it's neither too easy nor too frustrating.

  • Aligned with IGCSE, IB, and national curricula — every exercise has a clear learning goal
  • Difficulty adapts based on your child's demonstrated performance
  • Short, focused tasks (5–10 minutes) to maintain attention
  • Clear explanations when something goes wrong — not just "incorrect"
  • Cheat cards your child builds themselves as they learn

Parent dashboard

Full visibility into what your child practised, where they excel, and what needs attention. No guessing — real data, per subject, per child.

  • See what's completed, what's understood, and where they struggle
  • A clear learning path so children build skills step by step
  • Per-subject progress and mastery levels
  • Weekly trends and streak history
  • Invite a tutor for shared visibility

Tutor dashboard

Tutors see all assigned students, exactly where each child left off, and which exercises still need completing. Coordinate with parents seamlessly.

  • See where each child left off in their exercises
  • Track which topics are completed, in progress, or not started
  • Shared visibility with parents — same data, zero friction
  • Status indicators: on track or needs attention
