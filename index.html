The NoBox story

Scroll through the chapters that built this platform — from a family's need to a tool that grows with every child.

Chapter One

The walls around our plans

Every time we imagined a place where we would live for a while, there were walls that enclosed our plans.

School.

Every time the same worries: what if they fall behind, what if we damage their future? And so we always ended up in places that were compromises — and never our first choice.

Chapter Two

We searched

We searched for a platform that could travel with us. Something grounded in real learning science — that would challenge our children at exactly the right level and protect their curiosity rather than replace it with noise.

We found nothing that came close.

Chapter Two

If you cannot find it, build it

As a psychologist, I researched how people learn, remember, and stay motivated. For a while I taught them myself — until our oldest reached Year 8 and it became almost a full-time job. For one child.

We compiled our research — learning science, child development, international curricula — built the platform, and worked on it with our children until we were all satisfied. That is No Box Education.

Chapter Three

Our children became curious again

Not the performed curiosity of a classroom. Real curiosity — the kind that wakes up early and wants to know why the fishermen leave before dawn.

Academically strong. Often ahead. And the world became the lesson.

Chapter Four

The platform that builds itself

No Box Education is not finished. It never will be. Our children use it every day — and when something could be better, they tell us. The ones worth building, we build.

Our daughter was eight when she programmed her first game for the platform. Then a second. Then a third.

She did not do it because we asked her to. She did it because she had learned to believe: if you see something missing, you fill it.

That is what No Box Education is trying to do. Not just teach children what is already known — but grow children who build what does not yet exist.

How to get started

Three steps to a personalised learning journey designed around your child.

1

Complete your intake survey

Tell us about your child — their age, grade, strengths, and what they find challenging. This helps us understand where they are and where they want to go.

2

Personal chat with your student coach

After intake, you'll have a one-on-one conversation to align on learning goals, expectations, and your child's unique needs. No chatbots — a real person.

3

Your child starts their journey

We set up a personalised learning program. Your child logs in, starts practising, and the adaptive engine takes it from there — adjusting difficulty in real time.
